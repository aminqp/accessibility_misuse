Issue: Using Footer for Main Content

Issue 1: Using footer for main content

Website Title

About Our Company

This is the main content about our company, but it's incorrectly placed in a footer element.

Our company was founded in 2005 with the mission to provide excellent service...

Our Services

  • Service 1
  • Service 2
  • Service 3

Problem: The footer element is being used to contain main content rather than supplementary information. This misuse confuses screen reader users who expect footers to contain information like copyright, contact info, or links.

Correct Implementation

Website Title

About Our Company

This is the main content about our company.

Our company was founded in 2005 with the mission to provide excellent service...

Our Services

  • Service 1
  • Service 2
  • Service 3

© 2023 Company Name

Contact: info@example.com

Solution: Use the main element for the primary content of your page, and reserve the footer element for supplementary information that typically appears at the end of a section or page, such as copyright information, contact details, and secondary navigation.
