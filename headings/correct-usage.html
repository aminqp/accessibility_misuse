Correct Usage: Proper Heading Hierarchy

Correct Usage Example: Document with Proper Heading Structure

Company Website

Welcome to our company website. We provide excellent services and products.

About Us

Learn about our company history and mission.

Our History

Founded in 2005, we have been serving customers for over 15 years.

Our Mission

We aim to provide the highest quality products with exceptional customer service.

Products

Explore our range of products designed to meet your needs.

Product Category A

Information about our Category A products.

Featured Product

Details about our most popular product in Category A.

Product Category B

Information about our Category B products.

Contact Us

Get in touch with our team for more information.

Why this is good:

  • The page uses a single h1 element for the main page title
  • Major sections use h2 headings
  • Subsections use h3 headings
  • Further nested content uses h4 headings
  • The heading hierarchy creates a clear, logical document outline
  • No heading levels are skipped
  • Each heading clearly describes its associated content

Document Outline View:

├── h1: Company Website
│   ├── h2: About Us
│   │   ├── h3: Our History
│   │   └── h3: Our Mission
│   ├── h2: Products
│   │   ├── h3: Product Category A
│   │   │   └── h4: Featured Product
│   │   └── h3: Product Category B
│   └── h2: Contact Us

This outline structure provides clear navigation cues for screen reader users and helps all users understand the content organization.
