Issue: Nav Element with Non-Navigation Content

Issue 1: Nav element with non-navigation content

Website Title

About Our Company

Our company was founded in 2005 with a mission to provide excellent service to our customers.

We believe in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Main content goes here...

Problem: The nav element is being used for regular content rather than for navigation links. This misuse confuses screen reader users who expect nav elements to contain navigation links.

Correct Implementation

Website Title

About Our Company

Our company was founded in 2005 with a mission to provide excellent service to our customers.

We believe in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

  • Home
  • About
  • Services
  • Contact

Main content goes here...

Solution: Use the nav element only for navigation links, and use appropriate semantic elements like section for regular content. Add an aria-label to clarify the purpose of the navigation.
