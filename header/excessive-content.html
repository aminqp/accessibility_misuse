Issue: Header with Excessive Content

Issue 5: Header with excessive, non-header content

Website Title

Welcome to our website where we discuss many interesting topics.

Founded in 2010, our organization has been dedicated to...

Our mission is to provide the best information about...

Contact us at example@example.com

  • Home
  • About
  • Services
  • Contact

Problem: Overloading the header with too much content that should be elsewhere in the document structure makes it harder for users to find the main page content. A bloated header can create a poor user experience, especially for screen reader users who must navigate through all the header content before reaching the main content.

Correct Implementation

Website Title

  • Home
  • About
  • Services
  • Contact

Welcome

Welcome to our website where we discuss many interesting topics.

About Our Organization

Founded in 2010, our organization has been dedicated to...

Our mission is to provide the best information about...

Contact

Contact us at example@example.com

Solution: Keep header content focused on introductory elements (site title, logo, primary navigation, search) and move detailed content to appropriate sections in the main content area. This creates a cleaner structure that's easier for all users to navigate, especially those using assistive technologies.
